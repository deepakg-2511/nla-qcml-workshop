About the Workshop

Numerical Linear Algebra with Quantum Computing and Machine Learning Workshop

Welcome to the official website of the Numerical Linear Algebra with Quantum Computing and Machine Learning workshop. This event brings together leading experts, researchers, and enthusiasts to explore the intersection of cutting-edge quantum computing, advanced numerical methods, and machine learning.

As scientific computing evolves, the demand for efficient algorithms to solve complex numerical problems is greater than ever. This workshop aims to bridge the gap between classical numerical linear algebra techniques and the potential of quantum computing, enriched with machine learning approaches.

Over the course of this workshop, participants will delve into:

  • Quantum algorithms for linear algebra problems and their applications.
  • Numerical methods for solving high-dimensional systems.
  • Machine learning techniques for accelerating and optimizing numerical solutions.

This workshop aims to bring together experts from different communities working on tensor methods and their applications, which will serve as a basis for an interdisciplinary exchange of methods, ideas, and techniques, to develop a deeper understanding of tensor methods in machine learning, further advance existing approaches and enable new approaches to important problems.

The workshop will consist of a few contributed talks and poster sessions covering important work and recent developments in tensor methods in machine learning and data sciences. Furthermore, the workshop will include open discussion sessions to encourage the exchange of ideas and the development of a shared understanding of problems and methods among the workshop participants.

Whether you're a seasoned researcher or a curious beginner, this workshop will provide valuable insights and hands-on learning experiences. Join us for thought-provoking discussions, practical sessions, and a chance to network with professionals from academia and industry.
